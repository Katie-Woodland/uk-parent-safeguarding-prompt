# Quick Start Guide for Parents

**You don’t need any tech skills.**  
This guide will help you use the free AI prompt in under 5 minutes.

---

### Step-by-step Instructions

1. **Go to the Prompt**
   - Open this link: [AI-PROMPT.md](AI-prompt)
   - Click the **Raw** button (top right)
   - Select all the text and copy it (Ctrl+A then Ctrl+C)

2. **Start a New Chat**
   - Go to Grok (grok.x.ai), Claude (claude.ai), ChatGPT, or any other strong AI
   - Start a **completely new chat**
   - Paste the entire prompt you just copied

3. **Upload Your Documents**
   - Upload everything you have:
     - Emails from school / social services
     - Reports (CAMHS, educational psychologist, EHCP, etc.)
     - Attendance records
     - Your own timeline or notes
     - School policies they sent you
   - You can upload multiple files at once

4. **Tell the AI What to Do**
   Just type:
   > Start with Stage 1

   The AI will then guide you through the rest (Timeline → Policies → Analysis → Recommendations, etc.)

5. **Get Your Final Report**
   At the end you will receive a clean **Executive Summary** that you can copy into letters, complaints, or take to meetings.

---

### Tips for Best Results

- **Be organised** — Give files clear names (e.g. “01_Email_from_Head_15March.pdf”)
- **Start small** — You don’t need every document on day one. Add more later if needed.
- **Stay calm** — The prompt is designed to be 100% factual and professional. It removes emotion so your case looks stronger.
- **Review everything** — Always read the AI’s output before using it. This is a helpful tool, not a lawyer.
- **Add new files anytime** — Just upload more documents in the same chat and say “Update the timeline with these new files” or “Continue to Stage 3”.

---

### What This Tool Can Help With
- School refusing support for your child’s needs
- Unfair exclusions or attendance fines
- Misapplied labels (ADHD, autism, ODD, etc.)
- Social services or CAMHS not following proper process
- False allegations or poor handling of bullying/incidents
